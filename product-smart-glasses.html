Common Questions

How do I transfer videos to my phone?

▼
You can transfer videos instantly via the APEX App using the built-in Wi-Fi 6 connection, or connect the glasses directly to your computer using the provided USB-C cable.

Are the lenses prescription-ready?

▼
Yes! The Vision X frames are designed to be compatible with prescription lenses. You can take them to any local optician to have the lenses replaced with your specific prescription.

How long can I record on a single charge?

▼
You can record up to 90 minutes of continuous 4K video, or take over 2,000 photos. For audio-only usage, the battery lasts up to 8 hours.

Is there a recording indicator?

▼
Yes, for privacy and status, a small, discreet internal LED (visible only to the wearer) and a tiny external front-facing LED pulse slowly when recording is active.

Is it waterproof?

▼
The Vision X is IP67 rated, meaning it is fully protected against dust and can withstand being submerged in 1 meter of water for up to 30 minutes. It's perfect for rain and heavy sweat.
