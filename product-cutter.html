COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to replace the blade?

▼

Depending on usage, one SK5 blade typically lasts 5-10x longer than standard utility blades. For average DIY use, a single blade can last months. Professionals may change them every few weeks.

What materials can it cut?

▼

SnapCut Pro is designed to cut cardboard, leather, heavy fabric, plastic sheeting, vinyl flooring, and even thin metals like aluminum flashing.

Is it safe for beginners?

▼

Yes. The ergonomic anti-slip grip and auto-lock mechanism provide superior control, significantly reducing the risk of slips compared to cheaper tools.

Are replacement blades included?

▼

The Standard model includes 10 replacement blades. The Pro model includes 30 blades plus a hard carrying case. Our Pro+ model includes a lifetime blade subscription.

Is the warranty truly lifetime?

▼

Yes. We stand by our products. If the tool body or mechanism fails due to manufacturing defects, we will replace it free of charge, forever.
