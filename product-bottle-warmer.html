Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to leave plugged in?
Yes, the WarmBuddy features an automatic shut-off and overheat protection. However, we recommend unplugging it when not in use for long periods.
Which bottles does it fit?
The flexible neoprene sleeve fits almost all standard and wide-neck bottles, including Avent, Dr. Brown's, Tommee Tippee, and Comotomo.
How do I clean it?
The outer sleeve is made of durable neoprene that can be wiped down with a damp cloth. Do not submerge the electronic heating element in water.
Does it come with a power bank?
The WarmBuddy comes with a USB-C cable, but the power source (wall adapter, car charger, or power bank) is sold separately. It requires a 5V/2A output for optimal performance.
