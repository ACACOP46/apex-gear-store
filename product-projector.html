COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

Does it work with Netflix and Disney+? ▼
Yes! You can stream directly via WiFi casting or by connecting a streaming stick (like FireStick or Roku) to the HDMI port for the best experience.
How big can the projection actually go? ▼
The CineBeam can project up to 200 inches. For the best clarity and brightness, we recommend a size between 80 to 120 inches.
Can I use it outdoors? ▼
Absolutely! It's perfect for outdoor movie nights. Just ensure you have a power source and a relatively flat surface to project onto.
Do I need external speakers? ▼
The CineBeam features built-in Hi-Fi stereo speakers that are plenty loud for most rooms. You can also connect external speakers via Bluetooth or the 3.5mm Aux port for a true surround sound experience.
Is it compatible with gaming consoles? ▼
Yes, the CineBeam has a high-speed HDMI port that is compatible with PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and PCs, with minimal input lag for a great gaming experience.
